Get Ready to Fish

by Matthew McCrary

So from the looks of things spring is just right around the corner and summer will soon follow. With that in mind the current and upcoming weather looks like it's going to be a great fishing season for those involved. So taking that into consideration you can consider this article a friendly reminder to get all of your gear together and have everything ready first and foremost for "How Big Was That Fish Day" but also for any other events that may pop up. Because there's certainly some to follow and while I can't say anything offically as of yet it's always better to be prepared.

Now with your mind on being prepared allow me to present to you a small checklist of gear you might want to make sure is on hand. Bear in mind this is in no way a complete list but are certainly some key gear you should have on hand.

  • Fishing License
  • A fishing rod and reel
  • An extra line
  • A tacklebox
  • Some Hooks
  • Some Bobbers
  • Live Bait
  • Fishing Lures
  • Sunscreen
  • Sunglasses
  • Lip balm
  • Bug Repellant
