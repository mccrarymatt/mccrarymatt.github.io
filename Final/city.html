Starting a Business

Are you interested in starting a buisness well here are a few things you should do.

  • Do Market Research - Find out what's wanted in the area
  • Get a Business Plan - Use the information you gather to plan where you're going.
  • Figure Out Your Funding - Whether out of pocket or a loan you need to know you'll have enough to start.
  • Look At the Laws - Figure out what type of business works for you and any permits and licenses.
  • Plan Plan Plan - Besides what you've planned so far you'll need other plans for different situations and events.

This is just a small list and there's certainly more to it for further details check out sba.gov
