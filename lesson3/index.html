Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide accurate information regarding the weather to those in the area. This information is to be used to help both residents and visitors plan and be prepared for both future and current weather. It is the site's goal to provide this information in such a way that ensures the communities safety and enjoyment. As such information will be provided in the form of current weather, weather forecasts, and information regarding how the weather might impact local events and businesses.
