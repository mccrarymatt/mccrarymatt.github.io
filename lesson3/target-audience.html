Target Audience

Our audience consists of both individuals and families ranging from teens to older adults who are interested in outdoor activities and events that can be impacted by the weather. They may be local residents who are interested in how the weather is affecting their community or they could be visitors interested in local attractions and events. Both will will ultimately want to know how the weather will impact their own plans and how it is impacting the events and places locally.

Persona 1 : Sam

"We'll need to get an early start tomorrow if we're going to make it back before the storm rolls in" - Sam

Sam is a local guide for backpackers knowing the weather is essential to his work and knowing what sort of plans to make for those he guides. He'll often make plans in advance for those he's intending to lead and knowing the weather in advance is an important factor in wether to take a break or head home for the day. Beyond that he's a young father who likes using his knowledge of nature and the area to go on camping trips with his family so the outdoors is just as much a part of his recreation as it is work.

Persona 2 : Sarah

"That flower's only native to this area so I'd like to take a closer look." - Sarah

Sarah is a botanist who's visited the area for work in the past but has decided to return as a tourist. She's single and looking forward to the chance to relax without having to worry about work. Knowing how the weather might impact some of the places she want's to visit will play a major factor in what she decides to do for the day and even though she's not here for the plants that doesn't mean her natural curiosity will be sated wether here to relax or work it's the outdoors that brought her here in the first place.

Scenarios

  • Do we have to worry about rain for the community fair next week?
  • Will it be too foggy for me to go hikeing tomorrow?
  • Is it going to be warm enough for a picnic in the morning?
  • Any idea when the rain's going to let up?
  • What's the weather like for next week? Thinking about fishing if you're up for it.
